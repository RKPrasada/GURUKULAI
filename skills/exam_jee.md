## JEE Mains Exam Context

- Pattern: 90 questions (30 each Physics/Chemistry/Maths), 3 hours, -1 for wrong MCQ
- Sections: 20 MCQ + 10 numerical (no negative for numerical)
- Key focus: Conceptual clarity + formula application + time management
- Mathematics: Calculus and Coordinate Geometry carry highest weight
- Physics: Mechanics and Electrostatics most frequently tested
- Chemistry: Organic reactions and Physical Chemistry numericals critical
- Common traps: Sign errors in integration, unit confusion in physics, IUPAC naming in organic
