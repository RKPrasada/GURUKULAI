## NEET Exam Context

- Pattern: 200 questions (50 each Physics/Chemistry/Botany/Zoology), 3 hours 20 minutes
- Negative marking: -1 for wrong answer, +4 for correct
- Key focus: Biology is 50% of paper — highest ROI subject
- Biology: NCERT lines are frequently asked verbatim. Read NCERT 3 times minimum.
- Physics: Similar to JEE but lower difficulty. Focus on formulae and units.
- Chemistry: Organic > Inorganic > Physical in importance for NEET
- Common traps: Diagram-based questions in Biology, exception cases in taxonomy
