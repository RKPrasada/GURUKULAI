## RRB NTPC Exam Context

- Pattern: 100 questions, 90 minutes, 1/3 negative marking
- Sections: Mathematics (30Q), General Intelligence & Reasoning (30Q), General Awareness (40Q)
- Key focus: Speed + accuracy. Most questions solvable in under 60 seconds.
- Mathematics difficulty: Class 10 level. Shortcuts and tricks are essential.
- Reasoning: Pattern recognition, not deep logic. Practice elimination strategy.
- GK: Static + current affairs (last 6 months). NCERT-aligned.
- Common traps: Unit conversion errors, misread options, calculation shortcuts ignored
