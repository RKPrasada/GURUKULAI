## NDA Exam Context

- Pattern: Maths (120Q, 2.5h) + General Ability (150Q, 2.5h)
- Negative marking: -0.83 per wrong answer in Maths, -1.33 in GAT
- Key focus: Mathematics paper is Class 11-12 level (harder than RRB, easier than JEE)
- Mathematics: Matrices, Calculus, Probability, Statistics are high-yield
- General Ability: English grammar + Physics + Chemistry + GK
- GK: Heavy focus on defence news, Indian military history, current affairs
- Common traps: Probability questions with complex conditions, English comprehension speed
